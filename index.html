Our Story

A Tailor Who Treats Every Garment Like Her Own

For years, neighbors across Merced have trusted Celina's Alterations with everything from a quick hem to the most important dress of their lives. Owner Celia Beltran brings a careful eye and a steady hand to every stitch.

Whether you need a suit taken in, a gown fitted just right, or a beloved pair of jeans brought back to life, you'll get honest pricing, quick turnaround, and work that's done right the first time.
